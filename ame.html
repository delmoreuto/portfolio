Ametsuchi

Cover art for the song "Ametsuchi" by Shing02. The song was created for Japanese manufacterer Six Inc, to demonstrate their product, the Lyric Speaker.

The cover takes inspiration from the aesthetic of the actual product itself and incorporates a thecnological motif. The cover centers around the idea of words being created through technology. The type for the Japanese characters '天地' was created for the cover.
